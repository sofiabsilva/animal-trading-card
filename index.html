Domestic Pig

Pigs are as intelligent as a 3 year old toddler! They rank #4 in animal intelligence behind chimpanzees, dolphins and elephants.

  • Scientific Name: Sus scrofa domesticus
  • Length: 0.9 to 1.8 m
  • Lifespan: Natural - 10-15 years, Slaughtered - 6 months

Pigs are very clean animals. They like to keep their toilets far from their living or eating area. And forget the saying “sweating like a pig”, pigs can’t sweat! That’s why they bathe in water or mud to cool off.
